There are many key things to know about Internet Networking. Networking is important to understand how connection works throughout different devices and mediums such as websites and sending files. Reference models allow for standardization across network devices with heterogeneous architecture. We use reference models to communicate with heterogeneous devices regardless of their architecture. An example of this is the open system interface which uses seven layers, each with a specific function, to serve the layer above. Additionally, transmission control protocol connects different networks. It handles stream delivery service, sending and receiving buffers, bytes and segments, full duplex service, connection oriented service, and reliable service.
